Switching from Mailchimp to Constant Contact

⚠️
Watch Out

Failing to send a re-permission email within 14 days after importing your list to Constant Contact can result in lower engagement and compliance issues, as recipients may not recognize the new email source and may mark your emails as spam.

If you're moving from Mailchimp to Constant Contact, the migration is straightforward:

  1. Export your audience from Mailchimp as a CSV (Audience → All contacts → Export Audience).
  2. Sign up for Constant Contact (free 60-day trial) and import the CSV directly.
  3. Send a re-permission email to your imported list within 14 days: "We've moved to a new email tool — confirm you still want to hear from us."
  4. Rebuild your top 2-3 automations in Constant Contact. The setup is faster than you'd expect.
  5. Run both platforms in parallel for one cycle before fully migrating, so you can compare results.

Most small businesses complete the full migration in under a week.
